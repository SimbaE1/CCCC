About CCCC

The Children's Climate Change Committee (CCCC) empowers young people to lead the fight against climate change. Through education, advocacy, and community action, we give children and teens the tools and platform they need to create meaningful environmental change.

Our Mission: Every child deserves a voice in securing their future. We believe that young people are not just the leaders of tomorrow, but the changemakers of today.
